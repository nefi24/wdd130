Idaho Rafting

To provide unforgettable white-water rafting adventures that bring people closer to nature, inspire confidence, and create meaningful outdoor memories through safe, guided river experiences.

History

Our rafting company began as a simple family tradition here in Idaho, where we grew up exploring the river, learning its currents, and sharing weekend adventures together. What started with just two rafts and a handful of family members helping as guides soon grew into a small, trusted outfitter. Even as we’ve expanded, we remain proudly family-owned and committed to the same values we started with—safety, honesty, and a deep love for Idaho’s wild rivers. Today, we’re grateful to share the adventure with every guest who joins us on the water.
